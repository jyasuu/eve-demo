Use when the user asks about earnings, financial performance, or quarterly results.

## Workflow

1. `connection__yfinance__get_earnings` for historical earnings
2. `connection__yfinance__get_earnings_trend` for analyst estimates
3. `connection__yfinance__get_income_statement` for revenue and profit details
4. `connection__yfinance__get_balance_sheet` for financial health
5. `connection__yfinance__get_cashflow` for cash flow analysis
6. `connection__yfinance__get_calendar` for upcoming earnings dates

## What to Highlight

- Revenue growth (YoY and QoQ)
- EPS beats/misses vs estimates
- Margin trends (gross, operating, net)
- Free cash flow generation
- Debt levels and liquidity
- Upcoming earnings date and whisper number
